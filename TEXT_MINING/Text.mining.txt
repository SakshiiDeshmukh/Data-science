Text mining, also known as text analytics or natural language processing (NLP), 
is a field of study that focuses on extracting useful information and insights from unstructured textual data.
It involves applying various techniques and methods to analyze and interpret text data, uncover patterns, discover relationships, and gain valuable knowledge from the text.
Text mining has applications in various domains, including social media analysis, customer feedback analysis, market research, 
information retrieval, and many more. It enables businesses and researchers to derive valuable insights
from large volumes of textual data, making it a powerful tool for understanding and extracting knowledge from text.
